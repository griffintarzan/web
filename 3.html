Chae Won Kim

Kim Chae Won (Korean: 김채원; Japanese: キムチェウォン; or simply Chaewon) is a South Korean singer-songwriter currently under Woollim Entertainment and Off The Record Entertainment.

She is a member of the project girl group IZ*ONE and trainee group Woollim Rookie.
